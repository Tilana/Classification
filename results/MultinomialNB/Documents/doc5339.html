----------------- IN THE COURT OF APPEAL, FIJI ISLANDS ON APPEAL FROM THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI CIVIL APPEAL NO. ABU0076 OF 2007S (High Court Civil Action No. HBC 227 of 2005S) BETWEEN: VEER SASHI MANU SINGH Appellant AND: ATTORNEY GENERAL OF FIJI Respondent Coram: Pathik JA Scutt, JA Lloyd, JA Hearing: Thursday, 6th November 2008, Suva Counsel: Dr. S. D. Sahu Khan for the Appellant R. Green for the Respondent Date of Judgment: Friday, 14th November 2008, Suva JUDGMENT OF THE COURT [1] The appeal in this matter came on for hearing before us on 6th November 2008. At the end of the hearing Dr Sahu Khan, counsel for the appellant, indicated that he was likely to receive instructions from his client to discontinue his appeal. [2] In the circumstances we adjourned any further hearing of the matter until those anticipated instructions were received. [3] On 10 November 2008 the appellants solicitors filed a Notice of Discontinuance, duly signed and consented to by the respondent, there to be no order as to costs. [4] In the circumstances, we order that: (1) The appeal be dismissed. (2) The orders made by Winter J on 31 October 2007 be confirmed. (3) No order as to costs. Pathik, JA Scutt, JA Lloyd, JA Solicitors: Sahu Khan and Sahu Khan, Ba for the Appellant Office of the Attorney General Chambers, Lautoka for the Respondent
