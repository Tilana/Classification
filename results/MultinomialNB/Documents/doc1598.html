----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION Criminal Case No: HAC 001 of 2009 FIJI INDEPENDENT COMMISSION AGAINST CORRUPTION V JASWANT KUMAR Counsel: Ms L. Lagilevu & Ms E. Leweni for the FICAC Mr. R. Chaudhary for the Accused Hearing: 1st February 16th February 2010 Judgment: 25th February 2010 JUDGMENT The assessors have given unanimous opinions that the accused is guilty of abuse of office as charged in count 1. The assessors are also unanimous that the accused abused the authority of his office for gain. Their opinions are available on the evidence. I direct myself in accordance with my summing up and find the accused guilty of abuse of office as charged in count 1. I am satisfied beyond reasonable doubt that the accused abused the authority of his office for gain. He is convicted of felony under section 111 of the Penal Code. Daniel Goundar JUDGE At Suva 25th February 2010 Solicitors: Office of the Fiji Independent Commission Against Corruption for Prosecution Messrs. Gordon & Chaudhry for Accused
