AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION Criminal Case No: HAC127 of 2011 BETWEEN: THE STATE AND: BIJENDRA BEFORE : HON. MR. JUSTICE PAUL MADIGAN Counsel : Mr. L. Fotofili for the State Mr. T. Sharma for the accused Dates of hearing : 8 and 9 April, 2013 Date of judgment : 10 April 2013 JUDGMENT [1] Bijendra, you have been charged with the following offence: Representative Count Statement of Offence RAPE: Contrary to Section 207[1] and [2][a] of the Crimes Decree No. 44 of 2009. Particulars of Offence BIJENDRA on the 28th day of November 2010 at Nasinu in the Central Division had carnal knowledge of PRIYEKNA CHAND, without her consent. [2] To this count, you have been found guilty on the unanimous opinion of three assessors. In directing myself on my own summing up, I agree with the assessors and find you guilty of the offence and convict you accordingly. That is the judgment of the Court. Paul K. Madigan JUDGE
