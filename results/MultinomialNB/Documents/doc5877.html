IN THE MAGISTRATE'S COURT OF FIJI AT TAVEUNI Criminal Case No: - 43 /2013 STATE V ELIA BERABI For Prosecution : - Constable Lui Accused : - In person SENTENCE 1. ELIA BERABI, you were charged in this Court for the offence of Assault Occasioning Actual Bodily Harm contrary to section 275 of the Crimes Decree No.44 of 2009. 2. You waived right to counsel and pleaded guilty for this offence on 22 April 2013. You also admitted the summary of facts. 3. According to summary of facts you without any provocation you assaulted your wife on 03 Feb 2013 Tavuki Village, Taveuni causing her injuries. 4. The Court is satisfied about your plea and convicts you for this offence. The Law and the Tariff 5. The maximum penalty for the offence of Assault Occasioning Actual Bodily Harm offence is 05 years imprisonment. 6. In Sereka v State [2008] FJHC 88; HAM 027 it was held that the tariff for assault occasioning actual harm ranges from a suspended sentence where there is a degree of provocation and no weapon used, to 9 months imprisonment for the more serious cases of assault. 7. Considering the facts in this case I take 06 months as the starting point of your sentence Aggravating Factors 8. The victim is your wife making this a domestic violence offence. You assaulted her without any provocation from her side. These will be considered as aggravating factors and I add 03 months to reach 09 months. Mitigating Factors 9. You pleaded guilty in the first available opportunity and therefore are entitled for 1/3 reduction of your sentence. Now your sentence would be 06 months imprisonment. 10. You are a first offender and married with 05 children. You have reconciled with your wife. Also you seek forgiveness from this Court. For these mitigating factors another 02 months will be deducted from your sentence to reach 04 months imprisonment. 11. This Court is mindful that under section 26 (2) (b) of the Sentencing and Penalties Decree it can suspend a sentence which is below 02 years. 12. You are a first offender. You saved Court's resources by pleading guilty at the first chance you got. For these you will be given a chance to reform. 13. Therefore I sentence you to 04 months imprisonment for the Offence of Assault Occasioning Actual Bodily Harm and suspend that sentence for 02 years. 14. You have to be in good behavior during the next 02 year. If you commit any crime during that period you can be charged under section 28 of the Sentencing and Penalties Decree. 15. Also this Court grants a permanent domestic violence restraining order with standard non molestation conditions in favor of the victim. 16. 28 days to appeal 23 April 2013 H.S.P.Somaratne Resident Magistrate.
